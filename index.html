01 — Executive Summary
The Opportunity. The Thesis. The Decision.
India's AI education market is wide open. Times Internet is uniquely positioned to own it.
Strategic Context
  • →AI adoption inflection: 600M workforce, <2% AI literacy. Reskilling wave is now.
  • →Window: 12–18 months. After this, Coursera/upGrad/startups consolidate.
  • →No dominant player in applied AI education for India's business professionals.
  • →Times' unfair advantage: 300M audience, ET trust, enterprise relationships, editorial engine.
  • →Near-zero CAC: upGrad spends 40%+; Times acquires at 5–15%.
Core GTM Thesis
"Media-led, trust-backed, enterprise-funded AI education." Lead B2B for revenue, B2C for scale, B2G for legitimacy.

13 — Risk Assessment
Know the Failure Points.
🔴 High
Content Commoditization
Free AI tutorials everywhere.
Fix
Focus on cert value, not content. Badge = what people pay for.
🔴 High
Enterprise Sales Slow
3–6 month deal cycles.
Fix
Pre-commit 10 pilots from existing Times advertisers.
🔴 High
Quality Perception Gap
Media ≠ education brand.
Fix
Co-certify NASSCOM/IIM. Hire 3 star instructors. Production quality.
🔴 High
Internal Deprioritization
Shared-resource death.
Fix
Independent P&L, dedicated team, Board sponsor.
🟡 Medium
Govt Contract Delays
Unpredictable timelines.
Fix
Model B2G as upside, not baseline.
🟡 Medium
Feature Over Distribution
Building vs. selling.
Fix
70% Y1 on distribution. 30% product.

Africa
Fastest-growing young workforce. Zero quality AI training. Long-term asymmetric bet.
Why Global Works for Times
  • →NRI audience exists: ET/TOI readership in UAE, US, UK, Singapore. Trust transfer instant.
  • →English content is global: Metro India courses work in Singapore, Dubai. Zero extra cost.
  • →Enterprise pull: Infosys India → Infosys Singapore. B2B flywheel has global reach.
  • →B2G underrated: Gulf govts running AI skilling programs seeking partners. Times opens doors.
What NOT to Do
No US in Y1–3 — most competitive, highest CAC, lowest Times brand.
No early localization — UAE/SG run on India English.
No physical offices — digital-first until B2G justifies.
Asymmetric Opportunity
A single UAE AI Ministry partnership > 100K individual course sales in brand + revenue. Times' relationships make this a real conversation.
